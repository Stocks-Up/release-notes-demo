# Release Notes v0.1.2

### Stronger Security, Broader Reach, and Live Insights

This release brings two-factor authentication, support for African currencies, real-time dashboard updates, and a French interface — making Kulu more secure, more global, and more responsive.

**What's New**

🔐 **Account Security**
• Protect your account with two-factor authentication (2FA)
• Stay signed in with the new "Remember me" option — your session lasts up to 30 days
• Your login tokens are refreshed automatically so you're never interrupted unexpectedly

🌐 **Multi-Currency Support**
• Transact and report in 10 African currencies: Nigerian Naira, South African Rand, Kenyan Shilling, Ghanaian Cedi, Egyptian Pound, Moroccan Dirham, West and Central African CFA Francs, Tanzanian and Ugandan Shillings
• Currency amounts are formatted natively for each region — correct symbols, decimals, and spacing

📊 **Real-Time Dashboard**
• Your dashboard now refreshes live — see revenue and activity update the moment it happens
• Revenue breakdown shows performance across Subscriptions, Services, and Products side by side
• Export your dashboard as a PDF report with a single click

🌐 **French Interface**
• Kulu is now available in French — menus, labels, and currency formatting all adapt to your language preference

---

**Bug Fixes and Improvements**
• Improved CI pipeline reliability — the release automation now runs without manual permission prompts
