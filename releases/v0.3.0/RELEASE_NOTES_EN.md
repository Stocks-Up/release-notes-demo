# Release Notes 0.3.0

### Take full control of your notifications

This release gives you a dedicated space to manage exactly how and when you hear from Kulu — per channel, per category, all in one place.

**What's New**

🔔 **Notification Preferences**
• Set individual preferences for email, Slack, and in-app notifications separately
• Control alerts by category: release updates, weekly digest, and security alerts
• Your notification defaults are now included in your user profile settings

---

**Bug Fixes and Improvements**
• Notification settings are now consistently applied across all channels

As always, we're working to make Kulu better for you. Have feedback? Contact us at support@kulu.com
